About Migone

Migone.cc is an online directory for farmers in Grenada. Our aim is to allow users to connect with these farmers from anywhere, rather than searching for them yourselves! We take pride in our heritage and wish to reflect this in our colour scheme with hints of red, green and yellow!


As we build our community of farmers and vendors we wish to add more functionality to the online experience.


Coming soon:


  • Search bar


  • Quality guaranteed sticker: Supporting quality service and products is of paramount importance to us! As we build relationships with our local vendors, the products you receive will reflect these growing relationships


  • On-time delivery: While you can now request delivery through

    Whatsapp Web | WhatsApp Mobile

    delivery services will eventually be built into our website.
